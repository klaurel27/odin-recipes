Curry Chicken

Indian inspired curry chicken with jasmine rice, peas, and peppers. This is one of our go-to dishes - easy and delicious! Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Jasmine Rice
  • Jar of Curry Sauce
  • Frozen Grilled Chicken
  • Peas (fresh, canned, or frozen)
  • Peppers and Onion (we use a frozen pepper and onion mix, fresh also works)
  • Optional: Add carrots, spinich, or almost any other vegetable, garnish with cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Cook jasmine rice per package instructions
  2. Add peas to the boiling rice
  3. Cook chicken, peppers, and onion in a skillet
  4. Add jar of sauce to skillet
  5. Mix in cooked rice
  6. Enjoy!
