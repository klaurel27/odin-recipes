Peanut Noodles

This one has been a long time favorite, and is a pad-thai inspired peanut noodle dish. Make it as spicy as you wish - we love it an hope you do too!

Ingredients

  • Rice Noodles
  • Peanut Butter
  • Soy Sauce
  • Siracha
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Veggie of Choice
  • Protein of Choice

Instructions:

  1. Cook rice noodles per package instructions
  2. Add peanut butter, soy sauce, siracha, garlic, and ginger to sauce pan (or wok) and simmer until well combined
  3. Cook protein and veggies as desired
  4. When rice noodles, protein, and veggies are ready, add to sauce pan/wok
  5. Simmer to combine and coat noodles
  6. Garnish with peanuts, cilantro, basil, or lime
  7. Serve and enjoy!
