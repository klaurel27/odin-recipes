Cashew Rice

This one is a new favorite. It's a rice pilaf with cashews, and can be served with asparagus (or any fresh vegetable!) and some lemon juice. I also like to add a little spice, like red pepper flakes or a cajun spice blend.

Ingredients

  • Jasmine Rice
  • Broken Spheghetti
  • Lightly crushed cashews
  • Ghee (or any other favorite cooking oil)
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Broth
  • Asparagus (or any fresh veggie)

Instructions:

  1. Heat about 2tbs of oil in a medium sauce pan
  2. Add garlic, onion, and 2oz of uncooked, broken speghetti and simmer for approximately 5 minutes
  3. Add 1 cup of uncooked jasmine rice and stir until coated with oil
  4. Add 2 cups of broth and simmer for approximately 20 minutes (until broth is absorbed)
  5. Cook and cut veggie seperately as desired
  6. When rice is finished, add cooked rice to skillet, and mix in cashews
  7. Serve and enjoy!
